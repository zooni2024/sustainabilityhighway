# Why IAQ Matters

Poor air quality leads to Sick Building Syndrome (SBS).

## Key Pollutants
- **VOCs**: From paints and adhesives.
- **PM2.5/PM10**: Dust from sandstorms.

## Mitigation
High-efficiency filtration (MERV 13+) is essential in KSA to handle dust loads.
